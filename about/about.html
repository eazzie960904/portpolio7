About Hyejeeong's Design with Heart
Hello! I’m a passionate Web Designer and Web Publisher with a keen eye for clean design, intuitive user experiences, and pixel-perfect implementation. I specialize in turning ideas into visually appealing and functional websites that not only look great but also perform seamlessly across devices. With a strong background in both design and front-end development, I bridge the gap between creativity and code. I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design, and I enjoy working closely with clients and developers to bring digital visions to life.
I'm always curious about the latest web trends and love learning new technologies to stay ahead in the fast-evolving digital landscape. Whether it's designing user-friendly interfaces or publishing accessible, standards-compliant code, I take pride in every detail of my work. Let’s build something great together!

For me, creating a website isn’t just about visuals or code — it’s about telling a story, making someone feel something, and building spaces where people can connect, explore, and stay awhile. With a heart for design and hands that love to craft detail, I focus on clean layouts, smooth user experiences, and thoughtful interactions. I enjoy bringing calm and clarity to digital spaces — turning ideas into something not just functional, but meaningful. I’m constantly inspired by everyday moments, quiet beauty, and the people I get to work with. Whether it’s a simple homepage or a complex build, I pour care into every line of code and every pixel I place. Thanks for stopping by. I hope we can create something beautiful together.
I believe that good design speaks quietly, but deeply. As a web designer and publisher, I’m drawn to the harmony between beauty and usability. I care about how something looks, but I care just as much about how it feels — how it moves, responds, and connects with the person using it. My process is thoughtful and detail-oriented. From wireframes to the final line of code, I strive to build websites that are not only visually pleasing, but also calm, clear, and human-centered. In every project, I try to create something that feels honest — something that welcomes, guides, and gently leaves an impression.
